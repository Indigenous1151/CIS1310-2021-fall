The Red Queen
The Red Queen is a book written by Victoria Aveyard. It follows a society
split between people based on the color of their blood; red or silver. People with
silver blood hold the high class of society and also happen to have super powers,
whereas the people with red blood do not, at least until the main character Mare that is.
